Program Spotlight

Keynote Speaker, Officiating Officer & Master of Ceremonies

Honoring distinguished leaders who will guide the message, formal ceremony, and flow of the inauguration celebration.

Keynote Speaker

Hon. Madam Manty Tarawalli, MSc

Hon. Madam Manty Tarawalli, MSc serves as Minister of State, Office of the Vice President and Principal Assistant to Vice President Dr. Mohamed Juldeh Jalloh. A former Minister of Gender and Children's Affairs, she spearheaded the landmark Gender Equality and Women's Empowerment (GEWE) Act, 2022 and now oversees several national multi-sectoral initiatives, including the MCC Compact, SUN, and SLEITI. A graduate of the London School of Economics and the University of Salford, she is internationally recognized for her leadership in governance, gender equality, and sustainable development.

Officiating Officer

Chairman Desmond Pessima

Chairman Desmond Pessima is the Regional Chairman of SLPP North America, providing strategic leadership to the party’s chapters across the United States and Canada. A dedicated party organizer and advocate for diaspora engagement, he has championed unity, organizational growth, and stronger collaboration between SLPP North America and the party leadership in Sierra Leone.

Master of Ceremony

Christiana Ellie-Kenny

Public Relations Officer, New Jersey Chapter

Known as PRO-At-Large for her active support of sister chapters. She holds a master’s degree in Engineering and works in the pharmaceutical industry.
