Speakers & Leadership

Honoring Our Guests

We are honored to welcome the following distinguished guests to the podium this Inauguration weekend.

Madam Baindu Dassama Kamara
Keynote Speaker

Former Minister of Social Welfare of Sierra Leone and current Presidential Advisor on Social Welfare and Inclusion. During her tenure, she championed disability inclusion, child protection, and support for the elderly, launching national programs in partnership with the UN and international partners to advance social services across the country.

Amb. Amara Sheikh Mohamed Sowa
Sierra Leone's Ambassador to the United States

Appointed in 2024 as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the United States, with concurrent accreditation to Canada, Brazil, and several Caribbean and South American nations. He previously served as Deputy Permanent Representative to the United Nations and led Sierra Leone's Free Quality School Education Programme from 2018–2022.

Chairman Desmond Pessima
Chairman, SLPP North America · Presidential Advisor on Youth Affairs

Leads the Sierra Leone People's Party's North America region, coordinating chapters across the United States and Canada. A vocal advocate for diaspora engagement in national development, he has mobilized SLPPNA members around voter registration drives, party unity initiatives, and support for national leadership.
